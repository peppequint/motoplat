who are we?

The Motoplat brand stands for high quality test equipment. Premium European quality, modern technology and easy to use are the hallmarks of each and every Motoplat tester. We are committed to providing our customers with the most reliable and comprehensive testers and therefore we have tailored our products and provide high service to meet customers’ needs.

After years of silence, in 2013 Motoplat came back on the market with renewed test equipment. Still keeping in mind its reliability, sturdy look and simplicity, but now with the highest possible technology inside we developed 2 types of CV test benches, which can also be supplied with higher load.

Next to the well-known benches, we developed several small testers that are an addition to your current tester. This means you can now test modern ‘COM’ alternators using your old testing device. The same Motoplat series contains testers that can even diagnose a modern alternator when it is still mounted on the car.

Recently we expanded our range by adding the VC-14DS stator and rectifier tester and the VC-203D armature tester.
