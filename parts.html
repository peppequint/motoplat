pulley set

With the Motoplat pulley removal set VP-028 it is very easy to disassemble and assemble welded pulleys on rotor shafts. These are used on Valeo ST and IST start/stop units.

The cutting teeth of the end mill is coated with polycrystalline diamond, which withstands high abrasive wear and makes the VP-028 last longer than other tools.

Using the supplied fitting, the end mill can be connected to a standing drill to remove the welded surface on the pulley.

The VP-028 also comes with two different fittings that can be placed on the two types of ST-pulleys.
